Version 2.0.2

Version 2.0.2 - July 15th, 2023

(1) Multi Languages​

  • Now you can use FluxBuilder in your native language, making it even easier for your team to collaborate effectively.
  • ▶️ Video Demo - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N2wbiZkKONE

(2) FluxBuilder Colors​

The freedom to personalize the color of FluxBuilder according to your preferred choice.

(3) Grid View ⚡️​

By incorporating the grid view feature, FluxBuilder enhances the ease of designing your apps' UI, making it easy to align.

(4) Zoom In-Out 🔍​

  • You can quickly zoom in or out the emulator by using 2 fingers to zoom in or out of it.
  • ▶️ Video Demo - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k5r6yxoEiEs

(5) Features Configuration​

Ability to save features without activate license. Now you can create App Demos for your websites and save the features configuration For Free.

(6) Shopify​

Support new features of FluxStore Shopify app, include:

  • MultiCurrency 💰
  • Shopping Video Widget 💥
  • Judge.me Review ⚡️
  • ▶️ Video Demo - https://youtu.be/lwLH7E_iPRI

(7) New Category Layout​

  • A brand-new Layout alongside our existing 8 Category Layouts.
  • ▶️ Video Demo - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rk4F44z6xbY

(8) Video on Slider Banner​

  • Support mp4 video and Youtube for Banner Images Slider.

  • Upgrade sound and full-screen mode for mp4 video-player.

  • ▶️ Video Demo - https://youtu.be/4McbOBjQWn

(9) Shopping Video Widget​

  • Support video list view like Tiktok and Youtube Short with .mp4 file.

  • Load the video list (get from Product) from TabBar.

    ▶️ Video Demo - https://youtu.be/WG_txIAriR4

(10) Web version 💥​

  • New release on 25 July 2023.

  • Scrolling up and down on the left menu.

  • Multilingual on the left menu: use FluxBuilder in your native language.
