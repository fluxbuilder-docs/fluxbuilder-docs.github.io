GDPR Compliance

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) is highly recommended to be added to the apps because of its essential role. This feature will require the users' permission for the app's Privacy Policies before using the app or signing up for a new account.

Besides, Apple's new policy requires Account Deletion for released apps. In FluxStore apps, the Settings screen will have an option to allow Customers to remove accounts to protect their Data privacy.

GDPR Compliance Configuration​

GDPR message for Ads Apps​

Beginning January 16, 2024, Mobile Apps having Google AdSense or AdMob will be required to use a Consent Management Platform (CMP) that has been certified by Google when serving ads to users in the European Economic Area or the UK.

Create a GDPR Message for ads apps with the steps below:

  1. Go to https://apps.admob.com/v2/privacymessaging and click on GDPR Block

  2. Click on Create a GDPR message

![](/notion_imgs/192328758.webp)

  1. Click Select Apps, and select the app you want to show the GDPR message

  2. Select all the options again as you want or you can set them as examples, after that click Continue

  3. At the final step, you can review your settings, set the name of this GDPR message, and click Publish to complete the setup

That’s all :)
