Version 2.5

Last updated: May 08th, 2026

FluxBuilder.com | MacOS | Windows | Guide | Support | Video | Affiliate

We are excited to introduce FluxBuilder 2.5.0 (MacOS & Windows), packed with powerful new design capabilities, advanced marketplace integrations, and enhanced payment gateways to give you total control over your e-commerce application.

🚀 The Web version of FluxBuilder is coming soon! Get ready for a fresh, silky-smooth UI and much faster performance (especially on your first visit), featuring all the newest features. Stay tuned! ✨

(1) FiboSearch Integration 🔎​

Added support for FiboSearch - the most popular WooCommerce product search plugin. It gives your users a well-designed advanced AJAX search bar with live search suggestions.

  • Seamless integration with the FiboSearch (Ajax Search) plugin.
  • Displays instant search results while typing, without page reloads.
  • Improves search accuracy and product discovery for users.
  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > General > Search Product > turn on the Enable Ajax Search option.

(2) Auction Products Support 🤼​

Transform your WooCommerce app into a dynamic marketplace with the Auction feature! Customers can now place bids directly within the app.

  • Require installing the WooCommerce Auctions - WordPress Simple Auctions plugin on your website.
  • Displays key auction information such as current bid, starting price, bid increment, auction start/end time, and auction status.
  • Supports real-time bid updates when users refresh the product detail screen.
  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Cart & Checkout > Auction > turn on the Enable option.

(3) Rental Products Support ✨​

Enable Rental Products with dynamic date and duration calculations in the app.

  • Require installing the Rental Products for WooCommerce plugin on your website.
  • Allow customers to select rental dates directly on the product detail screen.
  • Rental availability is validated in real time, and pricing is calculated accurately throughout the cart and checkout flow.
  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Cart & Checkout > Rental > turn on the Enable option.

(4) Appointments Products Support 🎉​

  • WooCommerce Appointments support is now available for all plans. Build your booking app with ease today!
  • Install the Appointments for WooCommerce plugin on your website.
  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Cart & Checkout > WooCommerce Appointments > enter data.

(5) Hybrid Payment 💳​

Introduced a seamless new hybrid checkout flow combining native UI with targeted webview payments. Supported: WooCommerce, MultiVendor (Dokan/WCFM).

  • New checkout flow: Native checkout → enter address → select shipping → create order via API → app opens the payment page in a WebView (payment only, no full checkout page) → customer pays → order complete. This feature is used to replace the old MStore Checkout webview.
  • If the customer cancels payment, the order remains with Pending payment status — a Pay button is shown on the Order Confirmation and Order History screens
  • Enable this payment: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Payment Methods > WebView Payment > turn on the “Enable Native Checkout” and “Enable Hybrid Payment” options.

(6) Modempay Payment 💳​

Integrated Modempay as a native payment gateway supporting wallets, cards, and bank payment methods. Supported: WooCommerce | Currency: GMD only.

  • Plugin: https://docs.modempay.com/plugins/woocommerce
  • Get your keys from merchant.modempay.com/developers
  • Enable this payment: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Payment Methods > ModemPay Payment > turn on the Enable ModemPay option > enter data.

(7) Payment Splits in Dokan 💳​

Seamlessly manage your marketplace app with our new Dokan Split Payment support!

  • Enabling commission-based transactions where payments are automatically distributed between the marketplace owner and vendors.
  • Compatible with Dokan Pro and PayPal Marketplace/Stripe Connect modules.
  • Enable this payment: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Payment Methods > PayPal Payment > turn on the Enable PayPal option > enter data.

(8) WhatsApp Order Integration 💬​

Enabled direct-to-WhatsApp ordering for quicker, localized customer support.

  • Allow customers to send their order details directly via WhatsApp after a successful checkout.
  • Integrate seamlessly with the existing payment flow.
  • Message templates are fully customizable, with order information automatically populated for convenience.
  • Support Native payment only (not support webview payment).
  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Payment Methods > WhatsApp Order > enter data.

(9) TeraWallet Customization 💳​

TeraWallet is a digital wallet and credit system for WooCommerce, providing seamless payments through deposits, transfers, and purchases using wallet balances.

  • Added option to enable/disable wallet.
  • Allows customization of the TeraWallet name.
  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > Cart & Checkout > Tera Wallet > enter data.

(10) Self-Hosted Share Link 🔗​

Updated and optimized the logic for generating self-hosted share links and handling Universal Links/App Links.

  • Remove previous limitations in link creation, ensuring shared links always navigate to the intended screen regardless of layout configuration
  • Users can now generate and open share links correctly even when layouts are / are not tied to a specific category, brand, or tag.
  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > Features > General > Dynamic Link Config > in the Dynamic Link Service, select “Self Hosted” > enter data.

(11) New TabBar Templates 🌈​

Added new, beautiful pre-designed templates for the app's Tabbar to help you customize navigation faster.

(12) Sub-Category Depth Display​

Added a new UI layout that shows the depth and hierarchy for sub-categories, making navigation cleaner and more intuitive for your customers.

(13) Banner Video Configuration​

Introduced expanded settings to customize and control video playback specifically for the Banner layout.

(14) Card Height Adjustments​

  • Quilted Grid Tile: Added new controls to adjust card heights for a more balanced grid layout.

  • Top Product: Added custom card-height controls to improve alignment on the homepage.

(15) Image Positioning​

Added controls to adjust the image position and fit for the List Tile type of Product Horizontal layout.

  • Default: The image position is automatically adjusted based on the app language.
  • Image On Left: Displays images on the left side.
  • Image On Right: Displays images on the right side.

(16) Listing Detail Screen 💎​

Introduced new configuration options to customize the layout and details displayed on the Listing Detail screen.

(17) Booking History Control 💎​

You can now easily customize the Booking History item in the Profile (Settings) screen — show, hide, or reorder it just the way you like. Create your Listeo app today!

  • Enable this feature: create your app on FluxBuilder > on the simulator, select Profile (Settings screen) > on the right panel, turn on/off/reorder the Booking History item.

(18) Wishlist Enhancements​

Added new configuration options, including the ability to change the wishlist image and adjust the image BoxFit.

(19) Show Stock Quantity​

Added the ability to display the exact stock quantity directly on the Product Detail screen.

(20) Font & Text Scaling Settings​

Added Font Style and Max Text Scale settings into Theme & Logo configuration for improved accessibility and easy customization.

(21) Login Prompt for Webview Authentication​

You can now test and view the 'Required Login' screen directly on the simulator.

(22) Social Login on Registration​

Added a new toggle to display social login options on the registration screen for faster onboarding.

(23) Others​

  • Currency Setting Validation: Added strict validation for country codes within the currency settings to ensure data integrity and prevent payment errors (Shopify only).
  • iOS Auto-Build Fix: Resolved an issue during the iOS auto-build process that resulted in a "missing password" error.
  • Stripe v1 Removal: Completely removed all legacy Stripe v1 configuration.
