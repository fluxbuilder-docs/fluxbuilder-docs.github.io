YITH WooCommerce Badge Management

The feature creates eye-catching graphic badges to draw attention to special discounts, ongoing promotions, and the most important features of your products on app. These badges help customers quickly identify key information and make your listings stand out in the store.

The Badge feature will be available on app after installing the plugin Yith WooCommerce Badge Management on your website.

From the website admin dashboard, open Yith > Badge Management and add new badges for your app.

Note: There are some fields have not been supported for the badge, including Rotation__, Flip/Mirror text__, Set position and Scale on mobile__.

Then you can continue to set up the Badge Rules.

Note: currently the option Show badge to only specific users or user roles has not been supported on app.

In the next versions, General Settings of Badge Management will be applied to the app later.
