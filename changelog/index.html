Version 2.3.0​

Last updated: Oct 3rd, 2025

FluxBuilder.com | MacOS | Windows | Web | Support | Video | Affiliate

This release brings exciting new features, better performance, and more control over your app.

(1) AI Solutions 💥​

Our AI solution portfolio features the no-code AI App Builder and AI Chatbot, designed to help FluxBuilder users build apps faster.

  • AI App Builder: Create stunning apps by chatting with AI.
  • AI ChatBot:
    • Ask the AI to configure Features, adjust Design, or guide you step by step in creating your app.
    • Saves time and reduces setup complexity, especially for new users.

(Currently in Beta ❤️ Your feedback is welcome to help us make it even better!)

(2) FluxBuilder Dynamic Links 🌈​

FluxBuilder Dynamic Links is the top alternative to Firebase Dynamic Links. Fully integrated within FluxBuilder for maximum reliability, customization, and branding, it makes deep linking effortless.
  • Deep Link Sharing: Easily share product pages, categories, blogs, etc.
  • Smart Link Management: Easily create, edit, analyze, copy, and delete all links directly in FluxBuilder or through your app.

  • Real-Time Click Tracking: Analyze link performance and clicks instantly.

(3) Offline Mode 📶​

A new feature is added to keep your app running even without internet.

  • Show offline status clearly to users.
  • Cache loaded data for browsing without a connection.
  • Automatically reconnect and reload data when back online.
  • Essential for regions with unstable networks.

(4) Speed up: Optimole plugin ⚡​

Boost your app's loading speed by optimizing images with one of two dedicated plugins:

  • Optimole Plugin
  • Regenerate Thumbnail Plugin

(5) Loyalty Feature​

Implement a powerful loyalty program by offering customers the ability to earn offline points, encouraging engagement and guaranteed repeat business.

  • More details about the feature 👉 CLICK HERE
  • Available for Professional or Extended license.

(6) Point Offline Store​

A powerful feature that allows to generate QR codes automatically, enabling managers to scan and add or redeem points seamlessly.

Shop managers can scan QR codes, enter purchase amounts, and handle point transactions directly at stores. The app shows a digital points card with QR code in the user's profile for easy access.

  • More details about the feature 👉 CLICK HERE
  • Available for Professional or Extended license of WooCommerce or Multi-Vendor app.

(7) Login via Email OTP 🔐 Shopify​

Deliver a modern, passwordless login that prioritizes speed and security. Instant access guaranteed.

More details about the feature 👉 CLICK HERE

(8) Firebase Analytics Update​

Improved tracking for deeper insights and smarter decisions.

(9) Dynamic Links: New Solutions​

Due to Firebase's discontinuation of Dynamic Links, we've introduced smarter ways to handle deep linking and manage link sharing in your apps.

  • FluxBuilder Dynamic Links (Recommended)
  • Self-Hosted Sharing Link: share links from your own website for stronger branding.

(10) Removed: Firebase Dynamic Links ❌​

The service was discontinued from August 25th, 2025. This means that users who have recently created a Firebase account may not be able to create dynamic links for their accounts.

We recommend all users to use the above FluxBuilder Dynamic Links to avoid any disruptions in the dynamic link functionality. You can refer to documentation to see some methods to configure Dynamic Links in your apps.

(11) PWA Settings​

Customize Progressive Web App contact links for a smoother experience.

(12) Access Role for Webview​

Control what different user roles can see any webview item on the app.

Let users sign up as listing owners directly.

(14) AfterShip​

Shopify's CartAPI is officially implemented in FluxBuilder, as CheckoutAPI will be fully deprecated in April 2025.

While creating Shopify app, a user can choose the Version here.

Or update later in Builder Wizard.

tip

Please note that the current CartAPI does not yet fully support Native Payment functionality through ApplePay/GooglePay or Credit/Debit cards as CheckoutAPI does. Therefore, we recommend that users currently utilizing these payment features continue using the CheckoutAPI version. New users will need to implement CartAPI as CheckoutAPI is no longer available for new implementations. Shopify has indicated through their API previews that Native Payment functionality is under development for CartAPI, and we will continue to provide updates on this matter.
