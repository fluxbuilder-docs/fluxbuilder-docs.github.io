# Manual Guide


Product Details screen or Blog Details screen are easy to edit by following steps:


Step 1: on FluxBuilder, open your app, then click Home.


Step 2: Select a product/blog on the simulator.


Step 3: edit as you like.

## 2. Vertical Product Gallery


In addition to the normal product gallery, the app presents the product image gallery in a vertical format, adding a unique touch to the user interface of the product detail page.


The feature is now available for **Simple Type** and **Flat Style** of Product Detail Themes.


Follow the guide below to setup the feature:

- Step 1: open your app on FluxBuilder.
- Step 2: on the leftmenu, select the `Design` item. Then on the simulator, click on any product.
- Step 3: On the right panel, scroll to `Product Image Scroll Direction`. Then Select `Horizontally` or `Vertically`.

**Horizontally**:


![](/images/notion/80f152269c30.png)


**Vertically**:


![](/images/notion/8644dd982281.png)

## 6. Dynamic Product Variant


With this feature, the product variation options fields are transformed into visually appealing representations such as color, box, and option layouts. It is currently compatible with all apps.


Before setting the Product Variant layouts, you need to set up the **Attributes** of **Products** as the image.


![](/images/notion/84e412f4b88f.png)


Then, follow the steps below to change the layout in [FluxBuilder](https://www.fluxbuilder.com/download):


Step 1: Open your app.


Step 2: Select any product on the simulator.


Step 3: On the right side screen, scroll down to find the **Product Variant Layout.**


Step 4: Click **+** to add new **Attribute Slug**.


Step 5: Select **Layout** for the attribute.


![](/images/notion/10f5f4ea36dd.png)


### Attribute Layout


Currently, our apps support 2 layouts to display attributes:

- Attribute Layout is Normal

![](/images/notion/dccffcc61ac7.png)

- Attribute Layout is Inline

![](/images/notion/3c6b2f329b43.png)


Follow the steps below to configure in [FluxBuilder](https://www.fluxbuilder.com/download):


Step 1: Open your app.


Step 2: Select any product on the simulator.


Step 3: On the right side screen, find the **Attribute Layout** and select the suitable layout.

## 7. Variation Swatches


The Variation Swatches plugin turns the product variation select options fields into radio images, colors, and labels. Helps you show product variation items in images, colors, and labels. It also enables them in product quick view.


Besides creating size, brand, image, color, and label variation swatches, you can handle the swatches shape style Rounded and Circle.


It is available for WooCommerce, and Multi Vendors app. Compatible with this [https://wordpress.org/plugins/woo-variation-swatches](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woo-variation-swatches) plugin.


![](/images/notion/5e299dcc9734.png)


---


Follow these steps to set up variation swatches on app:


Step 1: Open Website admin, open **Products** and select **Attributes**


Step 2: Add new Attribute with **Image** Type


![](/images/notion/f2b607ab4a97.png)


Step 3: Click **Configure terms** to add items for the new attribute


Step 4: Re-open **Products**, then open the Variable product needing variation swatches


Step 5: In **Attributes**, select the created attribute and **Save attributes**


![](/images/notion/4b7b613b802b.png)


Step 6: Go to **Variations**, and add more variations


![](/images/notion/73ea59a9d8c1.png)


Now, the app will update automatically to show the image variations for the product.

## 9. Rating and Reviews


By reading reviews, customers can benefit from the firsthand experiences of others, gaining confidence in their purchasing choices. These apps - Woocommerce, Multi Vendors, Shopify and Listing - support rating and review presentations on the Product Details screen.


Use [**FluxBuilder**](https://www.fluxbuilder.com/download) to enable the feature as below:


💠 **For Woocommerce and Multi Vendors apps**


Step 1: Open your app.


Step 2: Navigate to the **Features** on Side Menu. In **General**, open **Product Review.**


Step 3: Turn on **Enable Review**.


![](/images/notion/3230a148a89b.png)


**💠 For Shopify app**


Follow the detailed guide [here](/18c6b0bf8db8448aa2b428bb6246286d).


💠 **For Listing app**


Step 1: Open your app.


Step 2: Navigate to the **Features** on Side Menu. In **General**, open **Product Review.**


Step 3: Turn on **Enable Review**.


Step 4: Open Design on the Side Menu.


Step 2: Select any listing on the simulator.


Step 6: On the right side screen, turn on **Enable Review.**

## 12. Size Guide Button


### 1. Plugin Size Guide Button


Ability to show the size guide/size chart on the Product Detail screen with the [Product Size Chart For WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/product-size-chart-for-woo/) plugin:

- Should use an image instead of an HTML table to show the guide chart because the app cannot load CSS from a website and apply it to show on the app.
- Require [MStore API 4.14.0](https://github.com/inspireui/mstore-api/commit/80cbc2e625a0d1eacc2066b413aaaa9b19cff27c) or [later](https://wordpress.org/plugins/mstore-api/).
- Support [theme](https://themeforest.net/item/woodmart-woocommerce-wordpress-theme/20264492).
- Available for Woocommerce, Multi Vendors.

The Size Guide will then be enabled on Product Details screen by following these steps:

1. Open Woocommerce/ Multi Vendors app on FluxBuilder.
2. On simulator, click on a product.
3. In **Size Guide Config**, turn on **Enable Size Guide**.
4. Input the **Attribute Names** that will display the Size Guide button.

**Note**: When a product fails to fetch the size guide html from the API, it will display a button and open webview with the **Custom Size Guide URL** that user adds to.


![](/images/notion/9f06c3b1c1d7.png)


### 2. URL Size Guide Button


For all apps, the Size Guide button works through Size Guide URL (which means plugin is not required).

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. On simulator, click on a product.
3. In **Size Guide Config**, turn on **Enable Size Guide**.
4. Input **Custom Size Guide URL**.
5. Add the **Attribute Names** that will display the Size Guide button.

![](/images/notion/376539c780b7.png)

## 15. Open Product in Webview


“Product Type to Open in Webview”: Gain full control over navigation behavior by choosing whether products open in **Webview** or **Native Product Detail** based on product type, ensuring consistent user experience.

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features**. In **General**, open **Product Card**.
3. In **Product Types to Open in Webview**, add the product types you want to open in webview.
4. Click **Save**.
