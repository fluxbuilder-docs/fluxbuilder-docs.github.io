title: "App UIs"
---


**Design other UIs of your App** easily and flexibly by drag&drop. 


You can also configure to [update your App’s UI without republishing app](https://docs.fluxbuilder.com/update-app-without-republishing) to App Store and Google Play.


<Tip>
Deliver and Manager apps do not support the following App UIs.
</Tip>
