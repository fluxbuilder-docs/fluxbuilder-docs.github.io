## 3. Order from Multi/Single Vendor


### Enable Multi/Single Vendor


On the app, you can add an option: Prevent users from adding products from multiple vendors.


![](/images/notion/e43bfff0ab5b.png)

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder
2. Go to **Features** → in **Cart & Checkout**, select **Vendors**
3. Disable **Multi Vendors Checkout**

### Multi Vendors Detected PopUp


The apps support showing a Multi Vendors Detected PopUp to remind users after preventing users from adding products from multiple stores.


![](/images/notion/b6e2592855cc.png)


Steps:

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features** > **Vendors**.
3. In **Checkout**, disable **Multi Vendors Checkout** and enable **Show Popup If Multi Vendors Detected**.

![](/images/notion/9413a0bf9624.png)


## 4. Vendor Admin


The Vendor Admin feature is available for WCFM only (when user login by vendor/admin account).


<Tip>
_You are also able to purchase the Manager app for this feature in native. This Vendor app is compatible with WooCommerce and WCFM plugin._
</Tip>


![](/images/notion/112860d28b8b.png)


To show Vendor Admin on app:

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. In **Design**, open the **Profile** tab.
3. Enable **Vendor Admin** in **GENERAL SETTING**.

![](/images/notion/357fb649d2d9.png)


### Vendor Admin (webview)

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features** > **Vendors**.
3. In **Vendor Admin**, disable **Native Store Management**.
4. Input **Vendor Dashboard URL**.
5. Select **New Product Status** as default.

![](/images/notion/de104a6e9abc.png)


This is an amazing feature to support Vendor Admin being able to manage everything related to their store. All the features will be the same as the features on the Vendor Admin website.


![](/images/notion/243cb98f47ba.png)


![](/images/notion/3a180767a83e.png)


![](/images/notion/7a906a359f5c.png)


### Vendor Admin (native)


As the current Admin is an All-In-One solution for Vendor admin on Multi Vendors app, it has a limit with WebView solution. For the Native vendor admin, you can combine with the Manager app that is full of native UX. 



![](/images/notion/08fe23e2cc78.png)

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features** > **Vendors**.
3. In **Vendor Admin**, enable **Native Store Management**.
4. Enable **Allow Publish Product** (vendors can publish products directly) or disable **Allow Publish Product** (products are asked to be reviewed before publishing).

![](/images/notion/1850d1405ad4.png)


## 5. Delivery


Users logging in with a **Delivery account** can directly access the **Delivery Management** page within the Multi Vendors app.


To show Delivery on app:

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. In **Design**, open the **Profile** tab.
3. Enable **Delivery** in **GENERAL SETTING**.

![](/images/notion/bbdf3dab626e.png)



After selecting **Delivery** on the **Settings** page of the Multi Vendors app, users will be directed to the **Delivery** page. You can configure the app to display the Delivery page in either **Webview** or **Native** mode.

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features** > **Vendors**.
3. In **Vendor Admin**, disable Native Delivery Management to open the Delivery webview page.
4. Input **Delivery URL**.

![](/images/notion/fb2516657970.png)


Note: If you already have Delivery app, you can enable Native Delivery Management to open the Delivery native page.


## 6. Listing: Allow Register as Owner


This update introduces support for owner role registration within the Listing app. Users can now register as business owners directly from the app interface.


![](/images/notion/acdd66f8b934.png)

- Open your Listing app on FluxBuilder.
- Go to **Features** on the side menu
- In **Cart & Checkout**, open Vendors.
- Enable the **Allow Register as Owner** option.

![](/images/notion/de89abe248de.png)
