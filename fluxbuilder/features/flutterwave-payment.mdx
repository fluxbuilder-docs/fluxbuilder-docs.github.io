title: "Flutterwave Payment"
---


**This payment is available for WooCommerce.**


<Tip>
Before adding this feature to your app, please make sure it is available on your website by installing [Flutterwave WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/rave-woocommerce-payment-gateway/).


Support Credit card, Debit card, and Bank account payments directly in your store.
</Tip>


Follow the steps below:


1. Follow [**this guide**](https://developer.flutterwave.com/docs/integration-guides/authentication/#api-keys) to get **API Key**.


2. On the FluxBuilder, open your app. Then select **Features**, enable **Flutterwave**, enter **Public Key.**
