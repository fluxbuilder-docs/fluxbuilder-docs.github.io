Midtrans - Payment Gateway of Indonesia has the ability to manage all payments and send funds (disbursement) easily.


The payment support Woocommerce, Pro (Woocommerce), and Multi Vendors apps.


## Configure Midtrans Payment


Before adding this feature to your app, please make sure it is available on your website by installing the plugin [Midtrans-Woocommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/midtrans-woocommerce/).

Then, open your app on **FluxBuilder**, click on **Features,** select **Midtrans Payment** option, and config as below:
