title: "PW WooCommerce Gift Cards"
---


This guide is to explain how to customize PW Gift Card products on app. Similar to Amazon.com gift cards, you can sell gift cards in your WooCommerce store.


The card number will show up when the order is completed.


![](/images/notion/3dd57c2b102a.jpg)


## 1. Prerequisite


The feature is available for WooCommerce, Pro (WooCommerce) and FluxStore Multi Vendors apps.


Before setting up the feature, you need to install the plugin [PW WooCommerce Gift Cards](https://wordpress.org/plugins/pw-woocommerce-gift-cards/) on your website:


## 2. Configuration


### 2.1. Enable Feature on App

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features**. In **General**, open **PW Gift Cards**.
3. Turn on **Enable** option.
4. Click **Save** button.

![](/images/notion/65df39e9d9c9.png)


### 2.2. Set Up the Website


After completing all the settings above, now let open your admin site, and follow the steps to make sure the feature works on app:
