title: "PayTM Payment"
---


**This payment is available for WooCommerce.**


You need to install [Paytm Payment Gateway](https://wordpress.org/plugins/paytm-payments/) plugin and enable it to work on your website. You need to config Merchant ID, Merchant Key, and select environment.


![](/images/notion/e5aaa3c8c106.png)


Then, enter `Merchant ID` to **Merchant Identifier**:


![](/images/notion/e8545a51ba79.png)


<Warning>
**Note**: the merchantId must be the same on your website and the production is must be the same as the environment on the website as well.
</Warning>
