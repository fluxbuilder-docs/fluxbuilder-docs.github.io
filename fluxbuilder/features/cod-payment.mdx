## Activate COD Payment on App


To get the feature, please make sure to **Enable Address Screen** on app as this guide. Then:

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features** > **Cash on Delivery Payment**.
3. Input the **Payment Method Ids**.

![](/images/notion/0194116dca06.png)


<Tip>
Note: If skipping this step, the COD payment still displays but the app will navigate to a webview payment page after users click **PLACE ORDER** button on **PAYMENT** screen.
</Tip>


## Update Order Status


As usual, the order status of COD payment is `Pending`. To change the status to `Processing`, please enable this option.

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features** > **Cash on Delivery Payment**.
3. Turn on **Update Order Status**.

![](/images/notion/05635e9bbf61.png)


## Smart COD


Set up charge fee for COD payment method directly on FluxBuilder.

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features** > **Cash on Delivery Payment**.
3. Turn on **Enable Smart COD**.
4. Input **Extra Fee** (the charge fee for COD payment).
5. Input **Amount Stop** (the maximum price applied COD charge).

![](/images/notion/9c236aedd6b8.png)
