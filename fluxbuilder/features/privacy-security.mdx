**GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) is highly recommended to be added to the apps because of its essential role. This feature will require the users' permission for the app's Privacy Policies before using the app or signing up for a new account.


Besides, [Apple's new policy](https://developer.apple.com/news/?id=12m75xbj) requires **Account Deletion** for released apps. In apps, the **Settings** screen will have an option to allow Customers to remove accounts to protect their Data privacy.

## GDPR message for Ads Apps


Beginning January 16, 2024, Mobile Apps having Google AdSense or AdMob will be required to use a [Consent Management Platform (CMP)](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/13554020?hl=en&ref_topic=10303737&sjid=15507369026803813240-EU) that has been certified by Google when serving ads to users in the European Economic Area or the UK.


![](/images/notion/76542f1c0ead.jpg)


**Create a GDPR Message for ads apps** with the steps below:


Step 1: Go to [https://apps.admob.com/v2/privacymessaging](https://apps.admob.com/v2/privacymessaging) and click on **GDPR** Block


![](/images/notion/c6e61bb5a0aa.png)


Step 2: Click on **Create a GDPR message**


![](/images/notion/21ed503c7766.jpg)


![](/images/notion/d92a40adf793.jpg)


Step 3: Click **Select Apps,** and select the app you want to show the GDPR message


![](/images/notion/5a7c51017896.jpg)


Step 4: Select all the options again as you want or you can set them as examples, after that click **Continue**


![](/images/notion/4617c7c64b9f.jpg)


Step 5: At the final step, you can review your settings, set the name of this GDPR message, and click **Publish** to complete the setup


![](/images/notion/35e2ad897b92.jpg)
