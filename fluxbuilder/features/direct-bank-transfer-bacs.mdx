---
title: "Direct Bank Transfer (BACS)"
---


If your website has Direct Bank Transfer (BACS) payment, this native payment will also be available on app.


The feature supports Woocommerce, Pro (Woocommerce), Multi Vendors, Notion, and Listing (Listeo) apps.


In case you want to use Multi Bank transfer methods, please install the following plugin into your website: [Fr Multi Bank Transfer Payment Gateways for WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/fr-multi-bank-transfer-payment-gateways-for-woocommerce/).


### Set up Direct Bank Transfer

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features**, select **Bank Transfer Payment** and config as below:
