The payment supports Woocommerce, Pro (Woocommerce), and Multi Vendors apps.


Ready to offer “Pay with FIB” for your customers, and see all successful transactions from your shop in the FIB app.


## Configure First Iraqi Bank Payment


FIRST IRAQI BANK payment is implemented based on this document


[https://documenter.getpostman.com/view/18377702/UVCB93tc#intro](https://documenter.getpostman.com/view/18377702/UVCB93tc#intro)


Then, use FluxBuilder to activate the payment on app.

1. Open app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features**, select **First Iraqi Bank Payment**.
3. Enable the feature and input all fields as below:

![](/images/notion/42b20b4bfb52.png)


That’s all!
