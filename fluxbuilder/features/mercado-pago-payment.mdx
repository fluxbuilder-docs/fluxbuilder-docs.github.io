---


Follow this guide to set up Mercado Pago Payment on your apps. The feature supports WooCommerce, Pro (WooComerce), Multi Vendors apps.


## Configuration

1. Install the plugin [Mercado Pago payment for WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce-mercadopago/) on your website, and make sure that it works.
2. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**, go to **Features**
3. Select **Checkout Screen**, open **SHIPPING** and turn on **Enable Shipping** option.

![](/images/notion/01a378a2cee0.png)

1. Open **MercadoPago Payment**, enable the feature, and fill in the required fields as below:

![](/images/notion/fa70593fff6d.png)


That’s all!
