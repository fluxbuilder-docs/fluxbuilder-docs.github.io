---
title: "Coupon"
---


This guide walks you through configuring the coupon feature in the app, including enabling coupon codes, displaying all available coupons in a list, and hiding expired ones.


![](/images/notion/b67cb80f35e7.png)


### Enable Coupon

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features** on the side menu.
3. In **Cart & Checkout** section, select **Coupon**.
4. Turn on the **Enable Coupon** option.

### Show All Coupon

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features** on the side menu.
3. In **Cart & Checkout** section, select **Coupon**.
4. Turn on the **Enable Coupon** option.
5. Turn on **Show Coupon List** to list all coupons when users click on the coupon box (turn off to require the user to type the code into the coupon box).
6. Turn on the **Show All Coupon** option to display all coupons of the app (turn off to display the available coupons for the user’s email).

### Show or Hide Expired Coupons

1. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
2. Go to **Features** on the side menu.
3. In **Cart & Checkout** section, select **Coupon**.
4. Turn on **Enable Coupon** and then turn on/off the **Show Expired Coupons** option.
