1. Login to your account in [razorpay.com](http://razorpay.com/).
2. Go to **Settings**, select **API Keys**, and copy the **Key Id** and **Key Secret**.
3. Open your app on FluxBuilder.
4. Go to **Features**, select **Razorpay Payment**.
5. Enable the Razorpay and input all fields as below:
