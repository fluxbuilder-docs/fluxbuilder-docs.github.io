Before adding this feature to your app, please make sure it is available on your website.


PhonePe - Payment Gateway has ability to enhance your users' payment experience with seamless transactions. With PhonePe, customers can easily make payments using UPI, debit/credit cards, and wallets, ensuring a quick and secure checkout process.


## Configure PhonePe Payment


Before the feature configuration below, please follow this guide to Enable Address Screen on app. 

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features** > **PhonePe Payment**.
3. Turn on **Enable PhonePe** and continue other fields as shown in the image.

![](/images/notion/0376ecd7162d.png)
