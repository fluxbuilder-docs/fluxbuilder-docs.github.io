## Enable Order History 

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Design** and open **Profile** page.
3. Enable **Order History** in **GENERAL SETTING**.

To view the option on the simulator, please enable **Login Mode**.


![](/images/notion/33484cd5ed43.png)


## Refund or Cancel


To allow users to cancel or return the product, you enable the Refund/Cancel feature. Specifically, the Refund button will be hidden once the refund period expires.


The feature is support in Woocommerce, Pro (Woocommerce or Magento), Multi Vendors, Strapi, and BigCommerce apps.


To setup the Refund/Cancel buttons on Order Details page, follow these steps:

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features** and open **Order History**.
3. Enable the option **Allow Refund/Cancel**.
4. Set **Refund Period** to hide buttons after dates.
5. Input payment method ids allowing refund/cancel feature in **Refund/Cancel for specific payments** (other payments will not support the feature).

![](/images/notion/15860f0828b9.png)


## Show Transaction Details


Allow to show or hide Transaction Details (only for Pending Order of the Direct Bank Transfer Payment or Thai PromptPay payments) in the Order History screen.


![](/images/notion/254e4a0b011e.png)

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features** and open **Order History**.
3. Enable **Show Transaction Details**.

![](/images/notion/3af12c0ef827.png)


## Show Re-Order Button


Allow to show or hide Re-Order button on the Order Detail screen.

1. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**.
2. Go to **Features** and open **Order History**.
3. Turn on **Enable Reorder** option.

![](/images/notion/2fdb8682330e.png)
