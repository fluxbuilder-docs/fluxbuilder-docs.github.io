---
title: "Nearest Stores"
---


Multi Vendors apps (using WCFM or Dokan plugin) can show the nearest stores on Google Map:


![](/images/notion/6d449fa4e5cb.png)


To enable the Nearest Stores, follow steps below:


## 1. Configure Google Map


Execute this guide [Google Maps](https://docs.fluxbuilder.com/7347e6a0-743f-41d2-bd73-6f08f560fa06) _(Skip it if you already did)_.


## 2. Configure FireStore Database


Follow these steps:


1. Create [FireStore Database](https://tppr.me/dSrwr) and select the nearest location to your customers.


2. Change the rule of [FireStore Database](https://tppr.me/1XJjO).


## 3. Edit on FluxBuilder


Set a tab on your app’s TabBar to Vendors layout:


Step 1: Open FluxBuilder, and open your Multi Vendors app.


Step 2: Click an item in TabBar. On the right panel, select the Vendors button in DESIGN LAYOUT.


Step 3: Choose one VENDOR LAYOUT.


![](/images/notion/595de938356a.png)


That’s all :)
