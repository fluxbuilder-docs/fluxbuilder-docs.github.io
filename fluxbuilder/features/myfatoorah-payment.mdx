Follow this guide to set up MyFatoorah Payment on your apps. The feature supports WooCommerce, Pro (WooComerce), Multi Vendors, and Listing apps.


## Configuration

1. Install the plugin [MyFatoorah - WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/myfatoorah-woocommerce/) on your website, and make sure that it works.
2. Open your app on **FluxBuilder**, go to **Features**
3. Select **Checkout Screen**, open ADDRESS and turn on **Enable Address** option.

![](/images/notion/ba50a3c9ea39.png)

1. Open **MyFatoorah Payment**, enable the feature, and fill all the required fields as below:
