### (3) Grid View ⚡️


By incorporating the grid view feature, FluxBuilder enhances the ease of designing your apps' UI, making it easy to align.

- You can quickly zoom in or out the emulator by using 2 fingers to zoom in or out of it.

Ability to save features without activate license.
