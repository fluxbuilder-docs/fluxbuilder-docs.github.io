### (10) Removed: Firebase Dynamic Links ❌


The service was discontinued from August 25th, 2025. This means that users who have recently created a Firebase account may not be able to create dynamic links for their accounts.


We recommend all users to use the above [**FluxBuilder Dynamic Links**](https://docs.fluxbuilder.com/fluxbuilder-dynamic-links) to avoid any disruptions in the dynamic link functionality. You can refer to [documentation](https://docs.fluxbuilder.com/dynamic-link/) to see some methods to configure Dynamic Links in your apps.
