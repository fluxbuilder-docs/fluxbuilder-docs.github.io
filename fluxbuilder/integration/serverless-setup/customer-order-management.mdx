## 1. Customers


All user information is displayed in this section. Admins can view detailed profiles and Orders of each customer.


![](/images/notion/e16eaf358f8f.png)

> For users who have deleted their accounts from the app, the **Status** column will show `Deleted`.

To remove a user manually, click the Delete icon button.


![](/images/notion/1014d6b7d87a.png)


## 2. Orders

> Track and manage incoming orders or service bookings, update statuses, and view transaction history

The Orders page displays both **Orders** and **Bookings** made by users through the app.


![](/images/notion/680745170d4d.png)


Click on any order or booking to view its full details and update the **Status** of the order/booking.


To remove an order/booking, click on the delete icon button.


![](/images/notion/652ab48457ce.png)
