---
title: "Sign Up and Login"
---


After launching FluxBuilder, sign up for a new account for Free, by selecting the **Sign In** item:


![](/images/notion/5764a0b865c1.png)


Select the **Sign In** as the image above. Then choose **Sign Up**.

- Input your name, email, and new password, then select **Sign Up**.
- Check your **mailbox/spam/ads/promotion** for the activation email, activate it. _If you_ <u>_do not receive any email_</u>_, on FluxBuilder please select_ <u>_Forgot Password_</u>_, you will receive an email to reset it._
- Then **Sign In** to FluxBuilder.
- **Issues and Solutions:** If nothing displays when you're typing, please change to English keyboard (typing in English)
