Welcome to FluxBuilder! FluxBuilder drag-and-drop app builder is a user-friendly tool that enables users to easily customize and build their own PWA, iOS, and Android mobile apps. It saves time and money by eliminating the need for a Flutter mobile app development team and months of waiting for app release.


FluxBuilder's main features are:

1. **Powerful Integrations**: Build diverse Apps effortlessly, including:
    - [Native app](https://docs.fluxbuilder.com/create-your-app) for your website. Compatible with top-tier eCommerce development platforms: WooCommerce, WCFM (WC Lovers), Dokan, Shopify, OpenCart, WordPress, Listeo, MyListing, ListingPro, Magento, PrestaShop, BigCommerce, Notion, Strapi, and even ChatGPT, etc.
    - [Webview app](https://youtu.be/wBRgB8C9_nI) for any website.
    - [Serverless app](https://docs.fluxbuilder.com/setup-serverless-store-in-admin-panel) (build apps without a site).
    - [Create Apps with AI](https://youtu.be/TiJCaAB7VEc).
2. **Flexible Design & Customization**: Choose from tons of stunning templates to create awesome apps.
3. **Instant Delivery**: Configurations will be pushed on Cloud Storage, upgrading apps without re-submitting them to Google Play or App Store.
4. **Cross-platform Tool**: Runs on Windows, MacOS, and Web version.

1. [Download and install](/6056d6e982d94d1180d17ce667ccf4e9) FluxBuilder
2. [Sign Up and Login](/22c6a812fe8943f197e15e60fafc2d90) to FluxBuilder
